Save Sea Puppies Foundation:

Protect sharks, Stop shark poaching, Save our apex predator

SAVE SEA PUPPIES FOUNDATION is a shark Organization is a passionate advocate for the protection of sharks worldwide. Through research, advocacy, and community engagement, we strive to raise awareness about the critical role sharks play in marine ecosystems. Our initiatives focus on combating overfishing, advocating for sustainable fishing practices, and promoting marine conservation policies to ensure the long-term survival of shark populations. With a dedicated team of scientists, conservationists, and activists, we work tirelessly to safeguard these majestic creatures for future generations. Additionally, we collaborate with local communities to implement shark sanctuaries and protected areas, fostering coexistence between sharks and humans. Our educational programs empower individuals to become stewards of the ocean, fostering a culture of respect and appreciation for sharks and their habitats.
